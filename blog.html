About This Space

A corner of the internet that's actually honest.

This is Baleigh's corner of the internet — a space for honest, everyday writing about life as a wife, a mom of four, and a woman figuring out what it means to follow God across the country to somewhere entirely new.

You won't find a perfectly curated life here. You'll find the laundry still on the table, the kids still loud, and faith that's real enough to hold up under all of it.
