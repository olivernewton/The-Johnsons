Why It Matters

Ministry work is not done alone.

Currently Tyler is on staff at Church of the Valley, Sandy, Utah in a part-time capacity. It is our hope that through the faithful generosity of many, he would be able to step away from nursing and pursue full-time ministry.

Since moving to Salt Lake and joining the church staff, we have seen many people come to faith and be baptized, an increase in first-time guests, and a true gospel awakening in our area. God is moving — and we want to be all in.

With your help, Tyler would be able to step into a full-time role and help the local church continue to grow, care for and invest in people, and pursue his call in ministry.

Behind every conversation, every relationship built, and every moment of true Gospel-sharing is a community of people who believed it was worth supporting — financially, prayerfully, and faithfully.

When you give, you're not just supporting a family. You're funding relationships, conversations, and moments of grace that reach further than we could ever go alone. You become part of every story being written here in Utah.

We are deeply grateful for every person who has believed in this calling enough to partner with us. Thank you for being part of what God is doing in the Greater Salt Lake Area.

— Tyler & Baleigh Johnson
