Why It Matters

Ministry work is not done alone.

Behind every conversation, every relationship built, and every moment of Gospel-sharing is a community of people who believed it was worth supporting — financially, prayerfully, and faithfully.

As full-time missionaries in the Greater Salt Lake Area, our family relies on the generous support of people like you to cover the costs of daily life and active ministry. From housing and groceries to outreach events and community resources, your giving goes directly to fueling this work on the ground.

No gift is too small. Every dollar is a statement of faith that says: this mission matters.

The Greater Salt Lake Area is home to more than a million people — and it's a place with a real and profound need for the hope found in the Gospel of Jesus Christ. We didn't come here because it was easy. We came because God called us, and because people here are worth showing up for.

When you give, you're not just supporting a family. You're funding relationships, conversations, and moments of grace that we couldn't create on our own. You become a part of every story that gets written here.

We are deeply grateful for every person who has believed in this calling enough to partner with us. Thank you for being part of what God is doing in Utah.

— Tyler & Baleigh Johnson
